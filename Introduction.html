Module 1:
Introduction to Databases

Introduction

In this module, you will be introduced to the basics of the database and learn about the advantages of using a database over traditional methods of storing data. You will learn about the challenges faced by companies who used traditional methods of storing data and how current technology has helped to overcome many challenges faced in the past.

This module discusses database management systems (DBMS) and their architecture. It also covers basic database terminology and fundamental concepts used in the industry. By the end of this module, you should be able to identify the properties and benefits of the database.

You will learn how databases are used in our day to day lives and how different corporations rely on a database for the proper functioning of their products.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you should be able to complete the following:

  1. Identify the database and its data structure.
  2. Relate knowledge of design theory to provide quality solutions in response to business database software development proposals.
  3. Describe the types of IT jobs that rely on knowledge of databases.
